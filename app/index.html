Our Wedding

Mario Portillo met Vivian Noriega online in May of 2014. After having promised Vivian coffee and tacos, Mario secured a first date. It was not much long after this that he fell madly in love with her.

He quickly requested she accompany him on his many adventures to come. She agreed. Now they are getting married and request you attend their wedding. A wedding to celebrate an upcoming life together undoubtedly filled with coffee and tacos.
